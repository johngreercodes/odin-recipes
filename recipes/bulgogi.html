Beef Bulgogi Bowls

Description

After my wife and I first got married, we bought a few weeks of Hello Fresh. One of the meals that stuck with us was this: the beef bulgogi bowl!

This is a nice and easy meal to make that incorporates some quick-pickled cucumbers. It's a good mix of heavy and light. Really everything you could want from a Bowl Of Stuffâ„¢!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Ground beef
  • Shredded carrots
  • Cucumbers
  • White vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Bulgogi sauce (just buy something pre-made)
  • Green onion
  • Pantry things (salt, pepper, oil, sesame seeds)

Steps

  1. Peel the cucumbers into ribbons and put them in a bowl. Add vinegar and sugar to the bowl. These will pickle while you make the rest of the meal
  2. Start the rice cooking (I just do stovetop, ratio of 1 rice : 1.5 water, add some butter at the end, leave it in the pot til ready to eat)
  3. Saute the carrots with some salt and pepper. Just for a few minutes. Then throw them in the oven on "Keep warm" while you make the beef
  4. Saute the whites of the green onion
  5. Add ground beef and saute until browned
  6. Once fully cooked, drain any beef fat, add a splash of vinegar and shushel for about a minute
  7. Pour in bulgogi sauce and continue to simmer over low heat
  8. Add sesame seeds
  9. Plate it up! Rice, beef, carrots, pickles, and top with the greens of the green onion
